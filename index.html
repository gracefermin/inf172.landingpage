About Your Host

Learn from a leading expert in behavioral psychology and digital behavior

Sean Young, PhD, MS

Executive Director of the University of California Institute for Prediction Technology, the Center for Digital Behavior, and Medical School Professor at UCI.

Dr. Young specializes in digital behavior and prediction technology—understanding how and why people use social media, mobile apps, and wearable devices. His work helps predict and change future behaviors in health, medicine, politics, and business.

As an internationally-recognized speaker, he has presented at the European Parliament, World Congress, and various corporations and academic institutions. He is a board member within the National Academies of Sciences, Engineering, and Medicine's Division of Health and Medicine.
